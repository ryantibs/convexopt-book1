\section{Structure of solutions}
\label{sec:lasso_structure}

Given a response vector $y \in \R^n$ and feature matrix $X \in \R^{n \times p}$,
the lasso problem is
\begin{equation}
\label{eq:lasso_primal3}
\minimize_\beta \quad \frac{1}{2} \|y - X \beta\|_2^2 + \lambda \|\beta\|_1. 
\end{equation}
The KKT conditions, which for this problem reduce to subgradient optimality, are  
\index{lasso!optimality conditions}
\begin{equation}
\label{eq:lasso_optimality2}
X^\T (y - X\beta) = \lambda s, \quad \text{where $s \in \partial
  \|\beta\|_1$}. 
\end{equation}
